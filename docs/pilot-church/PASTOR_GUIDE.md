# Pastor Guide — Ultimate Church OS

**For:** Senior Pastor, Lead Pastor, Associate Pastor (pastoral focus)  
**Sign in:** Church office → `https://<your-church-domain>/login`

---

## Your home screen

After sign-in you land on **Home** with a **pastoral** view of church health.

**First day card (four steps)** — use these until you know the system:

1. Review pastoral care queue  
2. Check members who need follow-up  
3. Review giving summaries (not voucher entry)  
4. Glance at upcoming events  

Dismiss the card when you are comfortable.

---

## What you will use most

| Module | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Home** | Overview, pastoral lens, shortcuts |
| **Pastoral Care** | Prayer requests, care cases, tasks |
| **Members** | Profiles, families, notes |
| **Giving** | Stewardship totals (treasurer handles vouchers) |
| **Reports** | Attendance and growth summaries |
| **Events** | Major church gatherings |

Your sidebar is focused on ministry — finance desk items are limited by design.

---

## Weekly rhythm

| When | Action |
|------|--------|
| Monday | Pastoral Care — clear queue, assign follow-ups |
| Midweek | Members — visitors and milestones |
| Before Sunday | Events / Sunday Service — confirm service plan |
| After Sunday | Attendance — review check-in (with admin team) |

---

## Key workflows

### Respond to pastoral need

1. **Pastoral Care** → open case or prayer item  
2. Assign task or note outcome  
3. Mark complete when followed up  

### Member conversation

1. **Members** → search name  
2. Open profile → review timeline  
3. Add note if appropriate (follow church policy)  

### Church health snapshot

1. **Home** → Executive or Pastoral lens  
2. **Reports** for trends
