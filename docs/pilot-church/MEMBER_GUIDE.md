# Member Guide — My Church Portal

**For:** Congregation members  
**Sign in:** `https://<your-church-domain>/member-login`  
**Not for staff** — staff use Church office (`/login`).

---

## What you can do

| Area | What you see |
|------|----------------|
| **Profile** | Your name and church connection |
| **Giving history** | Your recorded gifts |
| **Events** | Upcoming church events |
| **Attendance** | Your recent check-ins |
| **My groups** | Small groups you belong to |
| **Volunteer schedule** | Active serving roles |
| **Prayer requests** | Submit private requests to pastoral team |
| **Announcements** | Messages from church |
| **Sermons** | Recent published messages |
| **Church information** | Address, contact |

---

## First-time login

1. Open the link from your church (email or bulletin).  
2. Enter username and temporary password from the office.  
3. Change password when prompted (if your church enables it).  
4. Explore **Prayer requests** and **Giving history**.  

If you see “account not linked,” ask the church office to connect your login to your member profile.

---

## Submit a prayer request

1. Scroll to **Prayer requests**  
2. Type your request (private to pastoral team)  
3. Tap **Submit**  
4. Your recent requests appear below  

---

## Give online

Use **Give online** on the portal or visit `/donate` on the church website.

---

## Privacy

- Prayer requests are pastoral visibility — not public on the website.  
- Document downloads may require church office for sensitive files.  

---
