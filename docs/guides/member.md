# Member — Quick Start

## Sign in

1. Go to **My Church** sign-in: `/member-login`
2. Demo account (after `npm run seed:demo-church`): `member` / `demo123`
3. You arrive at **your portal** — not the staff church office.

## What you can see

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Profile | Your name and growth stage |
| Attendance | Recent check-ins |
| Giving | Your gift history + link to give online |
| Volunteers | Your serving roles |
| Announcements | Messages from your church |
| Events | Upcoming gatherings |

## Give online

Use **Give online** from the portal or visit `/donate` on the church website.

## Staff vs member

- **Members** use `/member-login` → portal.
- **Staff** use `/login` → church office.

If your account is not linked to a member profile, contact the church office.
