# Volunteer Coordinator — Quick Start

## First day (about 1.5 hours)

1. Sign in — lands on **Volunteers**.
2. Review active roles and assignments.
3. Open **Events** for upcoming services.
4. Open **Sunday Service** for live roster view.
5. Open **Attendance** for check-in sessions.

## First week

| When | Do |
|------|-----|
| Mid-week | Update rosters for Sunday |
| Saturday | Confirm volunteers in Sunday Service |
| Sunday | Attendance + live desk |

## Key workflows

- **Assign role:** Volunteers → assign → verify on member profile.
- **Sunday:** Quick bar → Live → Check-in → Team.

## Tips

- Mobile quick bar: Live, Check-in, Team, Alerts.
