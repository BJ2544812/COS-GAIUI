# Treasurer Guide — Ultimate Church OS

**For:** Finance Manager, Treasurer, Accountant  
**Sign in:** Church office → `/login`

---

## Your home screen

**Finance Manager** lands directly on **Finance** (vouchers tab).  
**Accountant** sees **Accounting desk** with a focused sidebar (giving, finance, budgets, vendors).

**First-day steps** on Home (if shown):

1. Voucher registry  
2. Giving ledger  
3. Budgets  
4. Reports  

---

## What you will use most

| Module | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Giving** | Record offerings, online gifts, receipts |
| **Finance** | Vouchers, approvals, posting, reversals |
| **Budgets** | Funds and budget lines |
| **Vendors & Payroll** | Bills and payables (with HR handoff) |
| **Reports** | Analytics and exports |
| **Compliance Documents** | Audit files (if enabled) |

---

## Separation of duties

| Role | Typical permissions |
|------|---------------------|
| Treasurer | Enter, approve, post vouchers |
| Accountant | Enter vouchers; treasurer approves |
| Pastor | View giving summaries — not voucher entry |

Configure in **Roles & Access** before go-live.

---

## Monthly rhythm

| Week | Focus |
|------|--------|
| Week 1 | Record all gifts; reconcile gateway |
| Week 2 | Payables and vendor bills |
| Week 3 | Budget vs actual review |
| Week 4 | Month-end reports; backup with admin |

---

## Key workflows

### Record Sunday offering

1. **Giving** → new gift  
2. Select fund, method, date  
3. Confirm GL linkage / receipt generated  

### Expense voucher

1. **Finance** → Vouchers → Create  
2. Attach details; submit for approval if required  
3. Post when approved  

### Reversal (correction)

1. Open original gift or voucher  
2. Use **Reverse** with reason  
3. Verify audit log entry  

### Month-end

1. **Finance** → reconciliation (if used)  
2. **Reports** → financial summary  
3. Export for council / auditor

## Pilot limitations (confirm with vendor)

- Email/SMS receipts may be in-app only until transport configured  
- Payroll may need pay structures configured in **HR** first  
- Opening balances should be imported before first live month
