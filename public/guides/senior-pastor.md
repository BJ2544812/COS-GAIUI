# Senior Pastor — Quick Start

## First day (about 1 hour)

1. Sign in at **Church office** (`/login`) with your staff account.
2. Open **Home** — use the **Pastoral** lens for people and care priorities.
3. Open **Pastoral Care** for cases and follow-up tasks.
4. Open **Members** to review profiles, milestones, and notes.
5. Open **Giving** for stewardship summaries (not voucher entry).

## First week

| Day | Focus |
|-----|--------|
| Mon | Review pastoral care queue |
| Wed | Check growth pathways and visitors |
| Sun | Glance at attendance and Sunday service desk |

## Key workflows

- **Care case:** Pastoral Care → intake → assign → complete task.
- **Member follow-up:** Members → profile → timeline note.
- **Church health:** Home → Executive view → Reports.

## Tips

- Use **Guide** in the header for a step-by-step walkthrough.
- Open **Academy** for your learning path.
